Difficulty Levels in Median XL Zeta

Just like regular Diablo II Lord of Destruction, Median XL features three difficulty levels. Killing Baal unlocks the next difficulty level for that character.

Hatred
Level Range: 1-55

The first and easiest difficulty level. Regular monsters' AI is not very bright and leech works at 1/4 effectiveness. There's no resist penalty. However, you still need a good amount of resistances, because starting from as soon as Act II, certain monsters deal heavy spell damage.

Terror
Level Range: 55-90

A significant step up in difficulty. Leech is reduced to 1/100 effectiveness, and there's a 50% resistances penalty. Monsters are smarter, deal heavier damage and have more hp.

Destruction
Level Range: 90-121

The hardest difficulty level. Leech is reduced to a mere 1/200 effectiveness, and there's a 100% penalty to resistances. Monsters are even more aggressive than before, and certain areas in the later three acts may prove to be much harder than even some uberquests. Monsters' spell damage is extremely high. The standard maximum of 75% to all resistances might not be enough, so you'll need to find items with a bonus to maximum resistances. Certain monsters might even pierce some of your resistances.
Make absolutely sure that you're prepared before facing the horrors that reside in the later acts of this difficulty.
