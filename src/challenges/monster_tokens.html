MONSTER TOKENS

Some targets on the battlefield may drop special items or tokens. They grant useful bonuses when cubed with the class charm you picked up in Kurast 3000 BA. Refer to the UBERLEVELS page if you do not have it yet.

VETERAN TOKENS

In Terror and Destruction difficulty, you can find veteran monsters near the end of each Act. There are 5 types of veteran monsters, one for each Act. Kill them and receive an 'Evil Eye' token.

Cube your class charm with all five tokens to receive additional bonuses.

[Class Charm] + each of the 5 tokens -> returns [Class Charm] with added bonuses

This upgrade recipe adds the following bonus:

+1 to [Your class] Skill Levels
+20% to Experience Gained

Veterans can be found in the following locations (number means act):

1. Clawstorm Terror: starting from Barracks and till Catacombs Level 3
2. Fairy Witch: Canyon of Magi
3. Glowing Blob: starting from Travincal and till Durance of Hate Level 2
4. Landmass: City of Damned, River of Flame
5. Bane Hunter: Worldstone Keep Level 1-3

ACT BOSS TOKENS

On Terror or Destruction difficulty, kill Act bosses to receive a 'Dogma' token. Collect each of the five tokens, plus two extra tokens corresponding to one of the Prime Evils (Mephisto, Diablo or Baal).

Then cube the tokens to receive a Signet of Skill. The Signet can be consumed to gain 1 skill point. You can only use 3 of these signets on any given character.

Required tokens (7) -> Signet of Skill
