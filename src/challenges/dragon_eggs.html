Dragon's Eggs

You might see these conspicuous eggs in low level areas. Kill them and the Baalspawn dragon will hatch. Killing this dragon brings great rewards... if you can kill it, that is.
Remember that Dragons attack with fire!

Your reward is a cube recipe
After you kill a dragon, you have 30 seconds to freely perform a cube recipe. These recipes are repeatable during this window of opportunity, so practice your button mashing skills!

Act 2

LOCATION: DESERT AREAS

Maximum character level to do recipes
Hatred: level 60
Terror: level 100
Destruction: unlimited

Reward
Killing the dragon allows you to reroll a rare item as many times as you wish! Until the 30 second timer runs out...

Rare item + town portal scroll -> reroll item as rare + town portal scroll

Item level of output item
Hatred: level 40
Terror: level 80
Destruction: level 99

Act 3

LOCATION: JUNGLE AREAS

Maximum character level to do recipes
Hatred: level 60
Terror: level 100
Destruction: unlimited

Reward
Killing the dragon gives you essentially an unlimited Chaos Reroll recipe for 30 seconds. This recipe will turn the item into a new item of the same type, which could be cracked, magical, unique or anything in between.
This recipe will never give you a sacred unique or set item.

Any item + identify scroll -> reroll item randomly + identify scroll

Item level of output item
Hatred: level 50
Terror: level 80
Destruction: level 99
