JEWELWORDS

Jewelwords are similar to runewords, but use jewels in all sockets.

To make a jewelword, use a grey (nonmagical, socketed) item and put jewels into all available sockets.

The resulting item will get the bonuses from the jewels and [+5 to all attributes & 5% to all resistances] multiplied by the socket count.

Example:

Grey Dagger (2 sockets) with 2 jewels
The item gets the bonuses from the jewels + 10 to all attributes + 10% to all resistances

The recipe also works with single-socket items, granting you the jewel bonus, in addition to +5 to all attributes and +5% to all resistances.

Jewelwords are a very powerful low level option for any character. If you come across a jewel while being a low level character, consider putting it in a grey item for a very useful bonus.
