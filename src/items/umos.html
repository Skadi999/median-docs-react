UNIQUE MYSTIC ORBS

As normal Mystic Orbs, Unique Mystic Orbs can be applied to proper items, but they add +10 Required Level to the item.

Usually one or two of them drop in Act 1 Hatred, and Heroic Boss Bull Prince Rodeo (see HEROIC LEVELS for more information) has 10% chance to drop one.
