GEMS AND RUNES

Median XL contains the standard Diablo II gems with new bonuses, but also 5 new gem types and 23 new runes.
Additionally, there are 6 Great Runes. They allow you to make the most powerful runewords in the game and drop from 2 difficult uberquests; Fauztinville and Toraja.
There are no cube recipes for creating, upgrading or downgrading Great Runes.
The Torajan Great Runes are generally considered more powerful than the Fauztinville ones, with Sha'ad being the most powerful and rarest rune in the game.
