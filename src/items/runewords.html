Runewords


Median XL contains 333 all-new runewords.

Single rune runewords
Each runeword contains only one rune, but you can fill up the sockets before the rune with jewels. To make a runeword in an item with 4 sockets, for example, you need to insert 3 jewels + 1 rune.

Grey items only!
Some people do not seem to know this. You can only make runewords in nonmagical socketed (grey) items!
